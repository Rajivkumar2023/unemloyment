Indian Navy Recruitment 2023

Vacancy
Indian Navy Recruitment 2023: Notification Out for 360+Vacancies, Check Posts, Age, Qualification, Pay Scale and Process to Apply Indian Navy Recruitment 2023: The Indian Navy is seeking applications from Indian Nationals for the engagement of ‘Tradesman Mate’ (TMM) at various units of Headquarters Andaman and Nicobar Command. As per the Indian Navy Recruitment 2023 official notification, the hiring will be made for 362 open vacancies which are classified into Tradesman Mate (338) and Tradesman Mate for NAD, Dollygunj (24). There are 52 ITI trades who are qualified to apply for the above-mentioned position. The distribution of openings in various categories may alter during the recruitment process as the overall number of vacancies fluctuates (increases/decreases). The age range for interested and eligible applicants would be 18 to 25 years old as of the closing date of application submission. The appointed candidates normally will have to serve in the units under the administrative control of Andaman and Nicobar Command, in Naval Units/ formations as per administrative requirements. As per the Indian Navy Recruitment 2023 official notification, shortlisted candidates will be given a monthly remuneration based on Pay Band as per Seventh CPC, Level 1 – Rs.18000-56900. Candidates should read and download the online information guidelines, which include instructions for completing the Online Application Form. Candidates must apply online using the online tag “Recruitment for the post of Tradesman Mate, Headquarters, Andaman and Nicobar Command” on the official website. The closing date of application submission is 25 Sep 2023 till 1700 hrs. Eligible candidates will be required to appear in a written test comprising objective-type questions based on the Essential credentials. The test will last for 02 (two) hours. The exact day, time, and location of the test will be sent to applicants via their registered cellphone number/email ID. The candidates must bring a print of the admit card to the written examination venue, failing which the candidate will not be allowed to appear for the examination. Candidates who arrive beyond the reporting time given on the Admit Card/call letter of Examination will not be allowed to take the examination. As per the Indian Navy Recruitment 2023 official notification, online application submission has started on 26 August 2023.

Indian Navy Recruitment 2023 Selection Process
The selection of the candidates for Tradesman Mate posts will consists of written examination, document verification and medical examination. In case of large number of application form received application will be short listed as per merit up to the ratio of 1:25 to the number of vacancies based on marks obtained in the minimum requisite educational qualification.
