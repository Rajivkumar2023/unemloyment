Indian Unemployment Rate 2023


Unemployment rate in India inched higher to 7.45% in February 2023 from 7.14% in January 2023, taking the total number of unemployed in the country to 33 million compared to 31.5 million in January. This is on the back of a significant increase in rural unemployment after it was on a decline in the preceding four months, the Centre for Monitoring Indian Economy said.
